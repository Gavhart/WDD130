Styles

The first thing to understand is style of riding, which style suits you the best? and what type of trail are you wanting to record? these are all goodd questions to consider when choosing your personal style. Enduro Enduro is a cross between cross country and Downhill style of riding, not only will you need a bike that can handle the climbs but also the descents. This style of riding you will take you not far but will be a fun ride. Downhill Downhill is a style of riding that is all about the descents, you will need a bike that can handle the descents and the jumps. This style of riding will take you to the top of the mountain and then you will ride down the mountain. Cross Country Cross country is a style of riding that is all about the climbs, you will need a bike that can handle the climbs and the descents. This style of riding will take you to the top of the mountain and then you will ride down the mountain. Trail Trail is a style of riding that is all about the descents, you will need a bike that can handle the descents and the jumps. This style of riding will take you to the top of the mountain and then you will ride down the mountain.
